Summary

Legislative Bill 316 introduces an excise tax of 10% on the retail sale of certain cannabidiol products, effective January 1, 2026. This taxation is in addition to existing state and local taxes. Retail sellers of cannabidiol products are required to maintain electronic sales records and submit them to the Department of Revenue upon request. Additionally, sellers must file a monthly tax return with the department, remitting the excise tax due. The Department of Revenue is responsible for collecting this tax and remitting the funds to the State Treasurer to be credited to the Property Tax Credit Cash Fund.
